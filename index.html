Nitte Mahalinga Adyantaya Memorial Institute of Technology (NMAMIT), Nitte, established in 1986, is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. It has been a constituent college of Nitte (Deemed to be University), Mangaluru since June 2022. Ranked in the 101-150 band in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2023 by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, NMAMIT has been placed in the ‘Platinum’ category for high industry linkages by the AICTE-CII Survey of Industry-Linked Technical Institutes 2020.

The Institute offers undergraduate programs in 15 engineering disciplines, including Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI&ML), Biotechnology (BT), Civil Engineering (CIV), Computer Science & Engineering (CS), and more. Seven of these programs are accredited by NBA, New Delhi, under Tier - I category until June 2025. The institute also offers postgraduate programs and has qualified research guides for Ph.D. candidates.

NMAMIT is located in a vibrant, serene, and green campus at Nitte, spread over 125 acres, nestled in the Western Ghats of Southern India. The nearest airport is Mangaluru International Airport (45 km), and the nearest railway stations are Udupi (40 km) and Mangaluru (50 km).
